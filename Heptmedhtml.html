Heptmed Pharmacy

Introduction

Hept med pharmacy is an ongoing project to deliver the best online pharmacy experience available in the market.

Features

Hept med pharmacy will have features that make shopping online for medicine and health related projects easier and with less hassle.
Some of the features it offers are:
1) Efficient sorting of products into categories for faster shopping.
2) Prescription upload for approving medicine that require prescriptions.
3) Easy booking of lab tests and doctor's appointments.
4) Easy access to all prior lab reports.
5) Medicine schedule upload for daily medicine reminders.
